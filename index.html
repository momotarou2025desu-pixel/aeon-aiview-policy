Privacy Policy (AeonAIView)

Last updated: 2025-12-29

1. Summary

AeonAIView is a launcher that opens AI service websites in an external browser. The app itself does not provide login or account management.

2. Advertising

This app displays ads provided by Google (e.g., AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK).

3. Information Collected

AeonAIView does not ask you to create an account and does not directly collect personal information like your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the advertising SDK may automatically collect and share certain information for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention, such as:

  • IP address (may be used to estimate general location)
  • Device and account identifiers (including Android Advertising ID and other identifiers, if applicable)
  • User product interactions (e.g., app launch, taps, video views)
  • Diagnostic information (e.g., crash logs / performance data)

4. How the App Uses Information

The app’s main function is to open selected websites in an external browser. Any login state, cookies, and browsing history are managed by the external browser and the third-party websites you visit.

5. Data Sharing

Ad-related data may be shared with Google and its advertising partners as part of ad delivery, measurement, analytics, and fraud prevention.

6. Your Choices

  • You can reset or delete your Android Advertising ID in your device settings.
  • You can manage ad personalization and privacy controls in Android settings and in Google’s ad settings.

7. Security

Data transmitted by the advertising SDK may be encrypted in transit (e.g., via TLS) as part of the SDK’s standard behavior.

8. Third-Party Services

Websites opened from AeonAIView are third-party services. Their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be published at the URL shown on the app’s store listing.
