AI Launcher Privacy Policy (by iogusnohin)

Last updated: January 20, 2026

1. AI Launcher Features

AI Launcher is a launcher app that can open selected AI service websites in an external browser (such as Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Firefox, etc.).

AI Launcher does not provide its own login system. Login status, cookies, browsing history, and any content you enter on those websites are handled by your external browser and the third-party websites you access.

2. One-time In-app Purchase

AI Launcher does not display ads.

This app offers a one-time in-app purchase to continue using the app after the trial period ends.

Payment processing is handled by Google Play. We do not obtain or store payment information such as credit card details.

3. Information We Collect

AI Launcher does not require you to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

AI Launcher does not collect or store website login credentials or chat content on our servers.

The app stores information such as the trial start time, the last date the guidance message was shown, and the purchase status (unlock flag) on your device to operate. This information is not sent to our servers.

4. Information Handled by Google Play

When you make an in-app purchase, Google Play may handle information necessary for payment processing, purchase verification, and fraud prevention.

We only receive the purchase result (e.g., whether you have purchased). We do not retain payment details.

For Google’s data handling practices, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. How We Use Information

The information described above may be used for the following purposes:

  • Providing the purchase feature (initiating purchases)
  • Checking and restoring purchase status
  • Maintaining and improving app functionality (minimum necessary)
  • Preventing fraudulent or abusive use (as needed)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not collect or share ad-related information.

In-app purchase processing is handled by Google Play.

7. Your Choices

  • You can manage purchases (purchase history, refunds, etc.) in Google Play settings.
  • If you change devices, please use “Restore Purchase” within the app.
  • You can review and manage device permissions and privacy settings at any time.

8. Permissions

AI Launcher does not request sensitive permissions such as access to contacts, phone/SMS, microphone, camera, or precise location.

If future versions require additional permissions, we will update this policy and explain why those permissions are needed.

9. Third-party Websites and Services

Websites opened from AI Launcher are third-party services. Their handling of data is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Please review the privacy policies of the websites you use and the external browser you choose.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

10. Children’s Privacy

AI Launcher does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page.
