AI Launcher Privacy Policy (by iogusnohin)

Last updated: 2026-01-22

1. Introduction

“AI Launcher” (the “App”) is a launcher application that opens selected AI services and other websites in your device’s external web browser (e.g., Chrome, Edge). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect or send the following information to the developer’s servers:

  • Personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email address
  • Login credentials (IDs, passwords, etc.)
  • Content you enter on websites opened in your external browser (e.g., chat messages)
Note: When you use third-party websites in your external browser, any login, input, cookies, browsing history, and related data are handled by your browser and the website provider under their own policies.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following information on your device only, to operate the App:

  • Trial-related information (e.g., trial start time)
  • In-app guidance/tutorial display status
  • Purchase status information (e.g., whether the App is unlocked)

The above information is stored only on your device and is not uploaded to the developer’s servers.

4. Purposes of Use

The information stored on your device is used for:

  • Managing the trial period
  • Applying usage restrictions based on purchase status
  • Providing screens and in-app guidance

5. Sharing / Third-Party Services

The App does not send the information stored on your device to the developer’s servers and does not sell personal information.

However, the following actions involve third-party services:

  • Network communication, display, and input on websites opened in your external browser (managed by your browser and the website provider)
  • In-app purchase processing (handled by Google Play)

6. In-App Purchases (One-Time Purchase)

The App offers a one-time purchase. Payment is processed by Google Play, and the developer does not receive or store your credit card information. The App may check your purchase status and may store purchase status on your device as needed.

7. Advertising and Analytics

The App does not use advertising SDKs or analytics tools.

8. Permissions

The App does not request sensitive permissions such as contacts, phone, SMS, microphone, camera, or location permissions.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended to collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, they will be posted on this page and the “Last updated” date will be updated.
