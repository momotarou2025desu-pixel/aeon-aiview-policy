Privacy Policy (AeonAIView)

Last updated: 2025-12-29

1. Who we are (Controller / App Provider)

This Privacy Policy describes how the app provider (“we”, “us”) handles information in connection with AeonAIView (the “App”).

Contact: momotarou2025desu@gmail.com

2. What the App does

AeonAIView is a launcher that helps you open AI service websites in an external browser (e.g., Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Firefox).

The App does not provide its own login system. Account login status, cookies, browsing history, and content you enter on websites (including chat content) are handled by the external browser and the third-party websites you visit.

3. Responsibility boundary (important)

  • Our responsibility: information we directly process inside the App (if any) as described in this policy. The App does not run our own backend server for user accounts.
  • Third-party responsibility: (a) advertising providers (e.g., Google/AdMob) and (b) the websites you visit and the external browser you use. Their data handling is governed by their own policies.

4. Information we collect directly

The App does not require you to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

We do not directly collect or store your website login credentials or your chat content.

The App may display ads provided by third-party advertising services (for example, Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK). Those providers (and their partners) may automatically collect, use, and share certain information for ad delivery, measurement, analytics, and fraud prevention.

Examples of information that may be processed by ad providers

  • IP address (may be used to estimate general location)
  • Advertising ID and other device identifiers
  • App activity / ad interactions (e.g., app launches, ad impressions, taps/clicks)
  • Diagnostics / performance data (e.g., crash logs)

Your ad choices

  • You can reset or delete your Android Advertising ID in device settings.
  • You can manage ad personalization and privacy controls in Android/Google settings.
  • Where required by law, ads may be shown only after you provide consent (and you can change your choice later).
